Introduction

Machines use power to apply forces and control movement to perform an action. ... Modern machines are complex systems that consist of structural elements, mechanisms and control components and include interfaces for convenient use.

Definition

A machine is any mechanical device that transmits or modifies energy to perform or assist in the performance of tasks. It normally requires some energy source ("input") and accomplishes some sort of work. ... A machine is anything that makes work easier. And also, a machine is essentially a device or tool which allows a force (or effect) applied at one point to overcome a resisting force (or load) at another point.
